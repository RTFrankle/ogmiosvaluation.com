Experience

Rob brings more than 15 years of valuation and corporate finance experience to every engagement. Prior to founding Ogmios, he served as Corporate Controller and Head of Portfolio Management at Fillmore Capital Partners, where he directed valuation oversight for a portfolio exceeding $3.5 billion in assets.

Earlier in his career, Rob held financial and operational roles at L Brands, Greif, and DSW — giving him direct, ground-level insight into the businesses he now values.

Outside the Office

Outside the office, Rob lives in Powell, Ohio with his wife, two kids, a Blue Heeler named Bandit — yes, after the dad in Bluey, and yes, the kids picked the name — and a cat he didn't ask for, but happy wife, happy life.

When he's not working, Rob can usually be found on the tennis court, or shuttling his kids to tennis and the pool.
