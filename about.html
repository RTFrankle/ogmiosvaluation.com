About

Built to be understood

A valuation is only as credible as the clarity with which it can be explained, the rigor with which it can be defended, and the trust it earns from the people who rely on it.

Our Name

Ogmios (og-MEE-os) was the Celtic god of eloquence — depicted not with weapons, but with a chain of gold and amber linking his tongue to the ears of those who followed him. He led not by force, but by reason.

We chose the name with purpose. A conclusion is only as strong as the clarity with which it can be explained, the rigor with which it can be defended, and the trust it earns from the people who rely on it. The numbers should tell a story. We build the argument around them.

Our Founding

Ogmios Valuation was founded on a straightforward premise: that a client should be able to understand their valuation — not just receive it.

We have completed more than 100 valuations across manufacturing, healthcare, retail, and real estate — from small privately held businesses to national operators with over $1 billion in revenue.

Our Approach

How we work

01
No Stake in the Outcome

We are retained to reach a supportable conclusion — not a convenient one. We are committed to the integrity of the analysis, and that independence is the foundation of everything we produce.

02
Built to Be Challenged

Every conclusion is documented, explainable, and stress-tested before it leaves our hands — whether it will face an IRS examiner, opposing counsel, or a skeptical board. We don't produce reports we can't defend in a room.

03
Senior Expertise, Start to Finish

Every engagement receives senior-level attention from start to finish. The person responsible for your conclusion is the person you speak with.
